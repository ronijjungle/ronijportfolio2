## The Challenge

Luxe Botanicals needed a brand identity that communicated luxury and purity without feeling sterile. The organic skincare market is crowded — the brand needed to stand apart while remaining authentic to its sustainable roots.

## The Approach

We started with an intensive brand strategy phase: positioning workshops, competitor audits, and audience research. This informed a visual direction rooted in natural textures, warm metallics, and editorial typography.

## The Result

A cohesive identity system spanning logo, color palette, typography, packaging, and a responsive e-commerce experience. The brand launched to strong press coverage and exceeded first-quarter revenue targets by 40%.
