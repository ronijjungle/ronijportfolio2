## The Challenge

Nova's existing site didn't reflect the caliber of their built work. They needed a digital presence that felt as considered and precise as their architecture.

## The Approach

We designed an editorial-style website that treats each project as a story. Large-format imagery, thoughtful typography, and cinematic scroll interactions create an immersive browsing experience.

## The Result

A high-performance website built with modern web technologies. Page load times dropped 60%, enquiry form submissions increased 85%, and the site was featured in multiple design publications.
