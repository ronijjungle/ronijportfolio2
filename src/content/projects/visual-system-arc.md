## The Challenge

Arc needed an identity that could flex across dozens of touchpoints — from massive stage backdrops to Instagram stories — while maintaining cohesion and recognizability.

## The Approach

We developed a generative identity system built around a core geometric motif that transforms based on context. A strict color and typography system ensures consistency, while the flexible mark provides endless variation.

## The Result

The identity was rolled out across all festival materials, social media, merchandise, and environmental signage. Attendance grew 30% year-over-year, with the rebrand cited as a key driver of renewed interest.
